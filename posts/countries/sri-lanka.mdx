Sri Lanka is Asia’s teardrop-shaped off-the-beaten-path paradise—home to palm-fringed beaches in Mirissa, rolling tea estates in Ella, and UNESCO World Heritage marvels from Sigiriya’s Lion Rock to the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy. You’ll chase waterfalls in Horton Plains, track wild elephants on a budget safari in Udawalawe, and feast on rice and curry plates for under $2—all without draining your wallet. With guesthouses starting at $5 a night, affordable coastal train rides, and vibrant festivals year-round, Sri Lanka delivers epic adventures and genuine local warmth at every turn.
