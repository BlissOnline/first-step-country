Vietnam is one of Southeast Asia’s most rewarding budget travel secrets—home to the glowing lantern-lit alleyways in Hoi An, and bustling street-food havens in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. You’ll slurp pho for under $1, glide through karst-studded Halong Bay on an affordable traditional junk boat, and navigate the floating markets of the Mekong Delta without ever blowing your wallet. From sunrise motorbike rides along the Hai Van Pass to sunset shrimp barbecues on sandy shores, Vietnam proves epic adventures can be astonishingly cheap and endlessly memorable.
