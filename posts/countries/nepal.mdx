Nepal is the ultimate playground for budget adventurers—towering Himalayan peaks, misty temples, and vibrant bazaars await at pocket-friendly prices. Imagine sipping chai at a Thamel street stall for under $1, chasing sunrise from Poon Hill on a $10 guided trek, and feasting on unlimited dhal bhat for $2. From UNESCO-listed Durbar Squares in Kathmandu and Bhaktapur to hidden monasteries in Mustang, every corner of Nepal delivers epic landscapes, cultural immersion, and genuine warmth—all without draining your travel fund.
