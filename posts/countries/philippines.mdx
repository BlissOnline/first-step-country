The Philippines is Southeast Asia’s wallet-friendly paradise—an archipelago of over 7,000 islands where turquoise lagoons in Palawan, emerald rice terraces in Banaue, and world-class surf breaks in Siargao await without breaking the bank. Feast on sizzling street barbecue and halo-halo dessert for under $2, hop cheap ferries between islands, and explore UNESCO heritage sites in Vigan and Manila’s Intramuros for next to nothing. With budget guesthouses from $10 a night, backpacker-style hostels from $5, and island-hopping tours starting at $15, this tropical playground proves that epic adventure doesn’t have to come with a scary price tag.
