Cambodia is one of Southeast Asia’s most captivating budget destinations—home to the ancient wonder of Angkor Wat, floating villages on Tonlé Sap Lake, and vibrant cities like Phnom Penh and Battambang. Feast on fragrant Khmer curries for under $2, cruise past jungle temples on a rented scooter, and meet locals whose warmth rivals the tropical climate. Whether you’re backpacking through Siem Reap or island‑hopping off the coast of Sihanoukville, Cambodia offers unforgettable experiences at prices that make long‑term travel possible
