From Bali’s emerald rice terraces and Java’s mist-cloaked volcano trails to the komodo dragons of Flores and the kaleidoscopic coral reefs of Raja Ampat, you can explore ancient temples at Borobudur for pocket change, savor a plate of spicy nasi goreng for under a dollar, and island-hop on cheap ferries—all without breaking the bank.
